Badge

Badge can be used to show either status of the user (online, or offline) or you can use it show notification count.

Badge on avatars

We have three types of badges to display user's status. Use online for status Online just like use offline , dnd for offline and dnd respectively.

Badge on icons

Here we have different classes secified for the alignment of the badge on the icon like top , right , bottom and left and for the numbered badge we have the class number-badge.
